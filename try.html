Sunyani School of Professional Studies

INTRODUCTION

SSPS has been certified to provide tuition for the Chartered Institute of Bankers, Ghana (CIB Ch)'s examinations using learning materials approved by the Institute.

Upon completion of the examination, one becomes a fully qualified chartered banker.

Entry Requirements

The minimum entry requirements for registration as a student at the institute are.
(i)School Certificate (6 Credits) or GCE 'O' Level with 6 credits in English Language and Mathematics, plus "A" Level in 3 subjects, 2 of which should not be below grade D.
(ii) WASSCE or SSSCE Holders; 3 core subjects including Core Mathematics and Core English plus 3 electives from either Business Programme or General Arts Programme
(iii) Minimum 5 credit passes at the General Business Certificate Exams including English Language and Mathematics plus 3 passes at the Advanced Business Certificate Exams including English Language and Mathematics plus 3 passes at the Advanced Business Certificates Exams.
(iv) Any other equivalent professional Certificate
(v) Holders of diploma or a degree from recognized tertiary institutions.
SSPS will provide tuition for all the four level for all the examination dates of April and October
